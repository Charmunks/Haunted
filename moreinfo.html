What is this?

Haunted is an event running until November 1st where you make a new theme or decoration for an existing website (one you made, or just a website you like) and get prizes! Bonus points if you make the theme auto enable every October!

What do I get?

Everyone who participates gets a custom Halloween themed sticker! Additionally, if you do at least 2 hours of work, you will get a grant to buy some Halloween candy!

What are the rules?

  1. You must be 18 or under.
  2. All work must be you own.
  3. Track your time using Hackatime.
